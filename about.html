Our Mission

Navkar Chain, the leading manufacturers and exporters of superior quality engineering products such as Sprocket, Chain, Pulley, Couplings, gears etc are today considered to be one of the industry's most dependable source.

Since our inception, Navkar Chain have always strived to be the leading and renowned supplier of superior quality products at the most competitive rates. It took us more then two decades, hard perspiring years to scale new heights and achieve a respectable position in the worldwide engineering Industry.

The company is equipped with facilities such as pattern shop, workshop, foundry and machineries of world renowned manufacturers such as WMW Germany, TOS Czechoslovakia, Pfauter, Rechards England and Indian reputed makes to product quality Sprockets, Pulleys & Gears. Each and every specification and technical details is kept in mind while manufacturing. Now we can manufacture and supply the product being suitable for Sugar, fertilizer, oil, cement, rice , paper , thermal power station, textile mills, chemical Industry, plastic industry etc.

At Navkar Chain , we take any type of customization of our products such as sprockets, pulleys, chain, gears etc as per customers drawing , specification or sample By continuous innovations and customer satisfaction we have obtain remarkable presence in domestic as well as overseas market. We at Navkar Chain manufacture and export Sprocket, Chain, Pulley, Couplings, gears etc to countries like China, USA, Europe, Latin America, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Far East, Middle East and others, according to the demand of international standard quality products, good price, prompt shipment and other services.
